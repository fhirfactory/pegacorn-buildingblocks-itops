## Storage needs Read/Write permissions
In Alpine container: (Yaml config files are in Aether-host-files | Azure repo)
mkdir -p data/prometheus-config
mkdir -p data/prometheus-storage
chmod 777 -R data/prometheus-storage
cp -rf /run/desktop/mnt/host/e/dev/aether-host-files/monitoring/* /data/prometheus-config

\helm\helm upgrade pegacorn-prometheus-itops-site-a --install --namespace site-a --set serviceName=pegacorn-prometheus-itops,imagePullPolicy=Never,basePort=32550,hostPathConfig=/data/prometheus-config,hostPathStorage=/data/prometheus-storage,imageTag=1.0.0-snapshot,numOfPods=1 helm
